TooLove

Airbrush Art & Stencil Tools

by Silke May

TooLove combines analog airbrush art with small digital stencil tools. The tools create cuttable stencil SVGs from images and text for plotters, airbrush, spray art and graphic stencil workflows.

LabeLove – Image to Stencil Text with Slots (macOS)

Input
Output with Slots

LabeLove converts text images into cuttable stencil SVGs with automatic slots. It is designed for lettering, logos and words that need to stay connected when cut.

Download for macOS

SobeLover – Graphic Edge Art from Images (macOS)

Input
Output

SobeLover creates bold, high-contrast edge visuals from images. It is useful for graphic experiments, stencil preparation and strong spray-art style effects.

Download for macOS

StenciLover – Cuttable Stencil Designs from Images (macOS)

Input
Output with Marks

StenciLover turns images into bold, simplified stencil designs. The goal is not photorealism, but clean, plakative and cuttable stencil artwork for real use.

Download for macOS

LabeLove – Text Stencil Tool (iOS)

LabeLove for iOS is a mobile text stencil tool. Unlike the macOS version, it works with typed text and creates stencil previews with slots.

Download for iOS

StenciLove – Multi-Layer Stencil App (iOS)

StenciLove for iOS creates bold multi-layer stencil designs from photos. It is made for quick mobile stencil drafts and graphic image reduction.

Download for iOS
